2.5 View Events Help

This section allows you to search and find Study Events that are a part of your current study based on your chosen parameters. You can choose the Study Event Definition, Status, Date Started, and/or Date Ended and click the button to display the results. You can also click on the Print icon next to the heading in order to print the results.

By default, a list of events will be displayed that happened in the current month in your current study/site. Subjects who were scheduled but with no data entry done past the date scheduled, are highlighted in yellow.
